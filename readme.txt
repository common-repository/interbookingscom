== Description ==

Adds the Interbookings.com bookings form to your post, or places the Interbookings.com button to your sidebar 

Just place your accesskey in the settings page and start receiving extra bookings.

1. Place the bookings form in any page you want
1. Place a good looking button in your sidebar to receive extra bookings
1. Has a simple, native interface
1. Always the most up-to-date information live from the Interbookings.com servers
 

== Installation ==

1. Install easily with the WordPress plugin control panel or manually download the plugin and upload the extracted folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Submit your accesskey in the Settings -> Interbookings.com page
1. Place the tag `{interbookings_form}` in your post to place the complete booking form, or simply add the Interbookings widget to your sidebar 

Place the Interbookings.com widget in your sidebar.

You can also use `{interbookings_form}` to place the complete bookings form in any page you want.
